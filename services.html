Services

A ladder, not a menu.

Most engagements start small. A focused diagnostic session to understand the situation, then a decision on where to go from there. Clients return for deeper work when the direction becomes clear, not before.

All engagements are led by Wei Kan. Execution runs through a vetted regional partner network for clients who need both strategy and delivery.

How We Work

Four ways in.

Most new relationships begin with the first rung. Clients with a clearly defined question and internal budget approval sometimes start directly at the second or third.
